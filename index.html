Privacy Policy

Namma Gadag – Travel & Tourism App

Last Updated: January 2026

About the App

Namma Gadag is an informational Travel & Tourism application designed to showcase the temples, forts, monuments, statues, and cultural heritage of Gadag district, Karnataka, in Kannada and English.

The app is built with an offline-first approach and prioritizes user privacy by design.

Information Collection & Use

Personal Information

Namma Gadag does NOT collect, store, share, or sell any personal information.

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Home or postal address
  • Location or GPS data
  • Photos, videos, or camera input
  • Device identifiers
  • Contacts, call logs, or messages

Your usage remains completely private.

Login & Profile

The app may display optional login or profile UI elements for presentation purposes only.

  • No real account creation
  • No authentication
  • No server communication

Any displayed profile information remains strictly on your device.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Works completely offline
  • No cloud servers or remote databases used

Internet Usage

The app does not require internet for core functionality. Internet access may occur only when you voluntarily open:

  • Support email links
  • External website links (future updates)

No personal data is transmitted.

Permissions Used

Permission Purpose Data Collected
Internet Support email / external links No data collected
Storage (Optional) Load local images & audio No data uploaded
The app does NOT use Camera, Microphone, GPS, Contacts, Bluetooth, SMS, or Call Logs.

Children’s Privacy

Namma Gadag is safe for all age groups and complies with COPPA and Google Play Families Policy.
